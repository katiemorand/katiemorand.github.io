Explination

I love animals. How could anyone not? They're so cute and so fun to play with. If I had the room and money to house all of the animals in the worls, I would. I tried to think of the top ten cutest animals to me and then I rated them on a scale of one to ten. One was the lowest and ten was the highest. I tried to use each number once, so I could rank them and put them in an order.

I truly think that all animals are cute, but not every animal can be the cutest. That is why I creaed this chart. I hope it will give light to my opinions on some of the cutest animals. This was very hard for me to do because I, personlly, think every snimal is cute. It was hard for me to first pick the top ten cutest animals in my opinion, but then I had to rank them and choose my absolute favorite. There are some animals that I would consdier equally cute, but I thought hard and picked one over the other.
